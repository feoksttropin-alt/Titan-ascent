--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PROJECT OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Titan Ascent is a physics-based rage climber. The player grapples up a living
titan from tail to crown (0–10,000 m). The codebase has 157 C# scripts covering
all major game systems. Most game logic is written; what remains is Unity scene
assembly, inspector wiring, platform integration, QA, and polish.

Engine:     Unity 6000.4.2f1 LTS
Platform:   PC (Steam)
Target FPS: 60 on GTX 1060 / RX 580
