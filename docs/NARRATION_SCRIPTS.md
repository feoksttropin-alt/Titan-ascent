# Titan Ascent — Narrator Script Bank

**Version:** 1.0  
**Narrator Voice:** Calm. Philosophical. Observant. Slightly sarcastic. Never panicked. Never cheering.  
**Cooldown:** Minimum 8 seconds between any two lines (enforced by `NarrationSystem`).  
**No-Repeat Rule:** The same line will not play twice in a row within a category.  
**Word Limit:** Under 12 words preferred. Hard cap 16 words.  
**Tone Note:** The narrator has seen this before. Many times. They are not unsympathetic — just measured.

---

## ClimbStart

> *Fires once at run begin. Sets tone. No sarcasm — this is invitation, not mockery.*

1. "The titan does not notice you. Yet."
2. "Another soul reaches for the crown."
3. "Gravity is patient."
4. "Thousands have tried. The titan remembers none of them."
5. "Begin."
6. "The titan breathes. You will learn its rhythm."
7. "Every meter from here was paid for by someone else's fall."
8. "The crown is up. Everything else is detail."
